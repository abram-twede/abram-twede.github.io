BU RISE

As a Course Instructor for All-In-AI in 2023, I have significantly contributed to this nonprofit's mission of offering high-quality educational content in machine learning and artificial intelligence. My role extends beyond traditional instruction; it encompasses empowering a diverse array of students, particularly focusing on underrepresented and underserved groups. I am actively involved in educating these students about the potential and applications of AI, helping to level the playing field in tech education...(click to read more)

One of my notable contributions is my involvement in the organization's summer camp. Each summer, we run a camp specifically designed to educate these groups about AI. This camp is unique in that it provides not only free, high-quality education but also sponsored food and computers, ensuring that resources are no barrier to learning. My role in this setting involves both instruction and mentorship, aiding high school students in securing tech internships and research roles, thereby opening doors to future opportunities in the tech industry. In addition to the summer camp, I have developed comprehensive Python tutorial videos for All-In-AI. These tutorials are crafted to cater to learners at all levels, from beginners to those advancing their skills, with a focus on practical application. Through these videos and interactive teaching sessions, I guide students through real-world scenarios and projects, enabling them to apply Python programming and AI concepts in practical contexts.
