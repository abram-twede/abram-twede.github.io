CPR Instructor

As a Red Cross CPR-certified instructor, I actively contribute to making my school a safer environment by certifying school athletic trainers, certified coaches, assistant coaches, teachers, and team managers. I provide them with essential training in CPR, Automated External Defibrillators (AED), and First Aid. My role involves conducting comprehensive training sessions that are both educational and hands-on, ensuring these crucial personnel are thoroughly equipped and certified to handle emergency situations effectively.... (click to read more)

Statistics show that sudden cardiac arrest is a leading cause of death in the United States, with nearly 356,000 out-of-hospital cardiac arrests occurring each year. Immediate and effective bystander CPR can double or triple a victim's chance of survival. Despite this, only about 46% of those experiencing out-of-hospital cardiac arrests receive the prompt help they need before professional assistance arrives. This training proves even more critical in athletic environments, where the probability of incidents needing immediate emergency response is heightened due to the physical nature of sports. I have also created and refined a YouTube tutorial that teaches basic CPR in Spanish.
